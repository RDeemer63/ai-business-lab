## Recording Note

If the contractor is comfortable, record the conversation (audio is fine). These recordings become training data for the objection library, sales scripts, and future demos. They are not for marketing.

## After All Three Demos

Gate 2 passes if 2 of 3 express strong interest.
Gate 2 is inconclusive if 1 of 3 expresses interest — run 2 more demos before calling it.
Gate 2 fails if 0 of 3 express genuine interest.
