# Research Department

**Mission:** Discover and evaluate business opportunities for the studio portfolio.

**Status:** Core agents permanent; specialist agents activate per workflow.

## Agents

| Agent | File | Status | Role |
|-------|------|--------|------|
| Business Research Agent | `custom/business-research.md` | Permanent | Idea scoring, market research, pipeline management |
| Business Strategist | `active/business-strategist.md` | Permanent | Strategy analysis, opportunity evaluation, competitive positioning |
| Trend Researcher | `library/agency-agents/product/product-trend-researcher.md` | Dormant | Market trend research; activates when a specific market needs investigation |

## Output directory

`01-business-ideas/` — all scored pipelines, decision briefs, and validated ideas

## Handoff

When Research produces a validated go signal, it hands off to the Sales department to design the offer and close the first client.

When Research produces a no-go signal, the Librarian records the result and the Business Research Agent updates the pipeline status.
