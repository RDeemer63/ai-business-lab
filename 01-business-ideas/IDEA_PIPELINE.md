# AI Business Lab — Idea Pipeline

**Scoring methodology:**
- C1, C4, C5, C6, C7, C9 scored at 1x weight
- C2, C3, C8, C10 scored at 1.5x weight
- Weighted total = (C1+C4+C5+C6+C7+C9) + (C2+C3+C8+C10) × 1.5
- Max possible score: 60

**Skepticism rule applied:** Ideas penalized when "automated delivery" still requires constant client contact, Ryan's personal reputation, or sales calls to function.

Ideas sorted highest to lowest weighted score.
