# Founder

**Role holder:** Ryan Deemer
**Role type:** Human -- not an AI role
**Function:** Direction, approval at human gates, portfolio decisions

---

## What this directory is

This directory documents the Founder role so that:

1. Future collaborators (partners, investors, advisors) can understand the decision framework
2. AI agents can reference the criteria when making recommendations
3. Ryan can review and update the criteria as the studio matures

The documents here are Ryan's judgment, formalized. They are not generated by AI. They represent how Ryan actually thinks about business decisions, investment, and risk.

---

## What the Founder does

The Founder sets direction and approves at defined gates. Everything else is delegated.

**Direction setting:**
- Chooses which business ideas to validate (from the pipeline)
- Approves the manifesto and operating principles
- Defines the kill criteria and investment criteria
- Adjusts strategy based on what the experiments produce

**Approval gates (always requires Ryan):**
- Any external communication (email, message, public post)
- Any financial commitment
- Selecting a business model to build
- Signing or representing any client contract
- Killing a business that has paying clients
- Adding a new department or permanently expanding the core team

**Review cadence:**
- NEXT_ACTIONS.md: as needed (when agents flag Needs Ryan)
- Business pipeline: weekly during active validation sprints
- Portfolio: monthly
- Architecture: when the Chief Architect requests review

---

## How to use this directory

**For AI agents:** When making a recommendation, check INVESTMENT_CRITERIA.md and KILL_CRITERIA.md before proposing that Ryan commit to or exit from a business. Match the recommendation to the documented criteria. If the criteria do not cover the situation, flag it for Ryan as a Needs Ryan item.

**For Ryan:** Review and update these documents at least once per quarter. Criteria that do not match how Ryan actually makes decisions are worse than no criteria. If a document says one thing and Ryan consistently decides another way, update the document.

**For future collaborators:** This directory explains the operating logic of the studio. A partner or investor reading these documents should understand how decisions are made and what the studio will and will not do.
